How to ask a Falsifiable Question

A falsifiable question is one in which a definitve answer can be attained. This is opposed to questions where the answer can be disputed.

Example of an Unfalsifiable Question:

'Was the 2020 US election rigged?',

this question can be disputed because there is no way to disprove the assertion that it was rigged.

Example of a Falsifiable Question:

'Will the US Supreme Court hear cases on election fraud before December 2021'

this question can be disputed because there is no way to disprove the assertion that it was rigged.

  • Ask yes/no type questions

  • Include deadlines in your question

  • What are the chances that Democrats will control the Congress after the 2020 elections?
